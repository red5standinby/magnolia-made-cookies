Our Story

Hi, I'm Laura.

I've been baking since I was old enough to hold a rolling pin — starting in my grandmother's kitchen in Hartford. A few years ago, I started making custom sugar cookies for my kids' birthdays. Alex wanted dinosaurs. Eliza wanted every flower in the yard.

What started as a weekend project became something I couldn't stop thinking about — the precision of royal icing, the way dough becomes art, the look on someone's face when they open the box. I'd love to make something beautiful for you.

— Laura
