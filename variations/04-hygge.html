Our Story

Hi, I'm Laura.

I've been baking since I was tall enough to reach the counter — standing in my grandmother's Hartford kitchen. A few years ago, I started making custom sugar cookies for my kids' birthdays.

What started as a weekend project became something more. The precision of royal icing. The way dough becomes art. The look on someone's face when they open the box. I'd love to make something beautiful for you.

— Laura
