Our Story

Hi, I'm Laura.

I've been baking since I was tall enough to reach the counter — starting in my grandmother's kitchen in Hartford. A few years ago, I started making custom sugar cookies for my kids' birthdays.

What began as a weekend project became a craft I couldn't put down. The precision of royal icing. The way a simple cookie can become art. I'd love to make something beautiful for you.

— Laura
