Our Story

Hi, I'm Laura!

I've been baking since I was tall enough to reach the counter. A few years ago, I started making custom sugar cookies for my kids' birthdays — and fell in love with the craft.

Every cookie is rolled, decorated, and packed by hand in our Connecticut kitchen. I'd love to make something beautiful for you.
