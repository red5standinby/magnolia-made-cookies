Meet the Baker

Hi, I’m Laura

I bake bespoke sugar cookies from my own kitchen — one small batch at a time. What began as a way to make my kids’ birthdays a little more special has become the thing I look forward to most: hand-icing tiny works of art for the people you love.

Every set is designed around your occasion, from the colours down to the smallest hand-piped detail. No two orders are ever quite the same — and that’s exactly how I like it.
