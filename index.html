Our Story

Hi, I'm Laura.

I've been baking since I was old enough to hold a rolling pin — standing on a step-stool in my grandmother's kitchen in Hartford, sneaking tastes of buttercream while her back was turned.

A few years ago, I started making custom sugar cookies for my kids' birthdays. Alex wanted dinosaurs. Eliza wanted every flower we could find in the backyard. What started as a weekend project slowly became something I couldn't stop thinking about — the precision of royal icing, the way a simple circle of dough could become something genuinely beautiful, the look on someone's face when they open the box.

Magnolia Made is my little corner of the world. I bake in small batches, I design every cookie by hand, and I genuinely care about making something you'd be proud to put on a table. Whether it's a birthday, a bridal shower, a holiday, or a Tuesday that deserves something sweet — I'd love to make something beautiful for you.

— Laura

What We Make

Artisanal Sugar Cookies,
Made to Order

Every cookie is rolled, cut, baked, and decorated by hand in our home kitchen. We use classic sugar cookie dough — buttery, vanilla-scented, just-right thickness — finished with hand-piped royal icing in custom designs. Nothing mass-produced. Nothing frozen. Just made-to-order cookies that look as good as they taste.
